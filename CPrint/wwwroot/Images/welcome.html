Welcome to Curries Print Centre

Curries Print Centre is a family run commercial Litho and digital printers. For over 35 years we have been meeting the printing needs of the Lothian's, Scotland and the UK with our personal and professional service. We provide full graphic design, print and marketing services for our customers from Large Organisations, Small Businesses, Charities and the members of the public

Design from Brief or existing artwork. Produce the highest quality products in a competitive market. Go the extra mile for our customers to be unique in their sector.

Company profile

In 1978 from a small garage in Broxburn, Brian Currie Printers was started specialising in Litho and Letterpress printing. In 1983 the company moved to its current site on Broxburn main street and established itself as a strong home grown company. In 1990 Brian was joined by his son Graeme Currie who learned the print business from the print room floor up to the design and management aspects.

In 2009 Brian retired passing the business to Graeme and with long standing staff and the drive to stay in the forefront of printing advancements the company has grown solidly over the years. We have now opened a 2nd unit locally which has allowed us to expand into large format printing, bespoke Lithographic and Digital printing and promotional printing. We are proud to have clients who are leading in their fields in Scotland and the UK.

What We Do

DIGITAL PRINTING - For small run print jobs digital is the way for you. With no film costs, plate making or drying time this is most advantageous for speed and time over traditional litho printing. With our digital printers including a brand new top of the range Ricoh on site we can print from 1 copy of your artwork to thousands on up to 300gsm paper. Thus allowing us to turn your work around in hours rather than days. LITHOGRAPHIC PRINTING - The more traditional way of printing is Lithographic. Although it has extra cost such as set up charges for plates and time for drying it allows for larger runs, Pantone spot colours, florescent inks, metallic inks and print onto heavier boards up to 400gsm. Our litho machines include a Heidelberg Speedmaster 2 colour and Presstek DI which uses waterless ink (Aqualess). Large Format Printing - We also can print large format images onto many mediums. From Wallpaper, Pop Up Banners, Canvas, Banners, Acetate to vinyl we can print it!

DESIGN - Our in house design team with over 20 years experience can design your stationary, logo, brand identity. Professional and current with the latest trends we will keep you ahead of the market. Being Green

Being green is extremely important to us and we love to encourage our customers to be too.

We are proud to be a member of the Woodland Trust Carbon Capture Programme and offer this opportunity to our customers.

We allow our customers who opt in to use our unique Carbon Capture logo on their print to show they too are striving to keep our planet safe by helping to replant our forests here in the UK and offsetting their carbon footprint from the products they order.
